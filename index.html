Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many aircraft I can add? ↓

No. Crosswind allows you to calculate envelopes for an unlimited fleet of aircraft with independent weight configurations.

Can I share my load plans? ↓

Yes. All load plans can be exported as professional PDFs and shared instantly via the native iOS Share Sheet or backed up straight to the Files app.

Are more templates and models coming? ↓

Yes. Profiles are continuously updated. Feel free to contact loadplanapp.lr@icloud.com directly with structural profile requests.
