FAQ

Is there a limit to how many aircraft I can add? ↓

No. Loadplan allows you to calculate loadplans for an unlimited fleet of aircraft with individual weight configurations.

Can I share my load plans? ↓

Yes. All load plans can be exported as professional PDFs and shared via the native iOS Share Sheet or saved directly to the Files app.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08 May 2026

At Loadplan, we believe your flight data should belong to you. Our app is built on a "local-first" philosophy. Any information you enter—including aircraft specifications, weight and balance data, flight schedules, and maintenance logs—is stored directly on your device. On-Device Storage: All data is managed locally using secure on-device databases. No Cloud Uploads: Your data is never uploaded to external servers, databases, or cloud services controlled by the developer. No Third-Party Access: Because the data never leaves your device, it cannot be seen, accessed, or sold to any third parties.

Document Generation and Sharing The app allows you to generate PDF reports and load plans. Local PDF Generation: These documents are created entirely on your device. User-Initiated Sharing: Files are only shared when you explicitly trigger the system Share Sheet. You have full control over where these files are sent (e.g., to your Files app, email, or a local printer).

Deletion: You can modify or delete your data within the app at any time. Uninstallation: If you delete the application from your device, all associated data stored within the app’s local container will be removed permanently, unless you have manually saved exported PDFs to your device's Files app.

The app may request specific device permissions (such as access to the Files app or Photo Library) solely to save generated documents or import relevant data. These permissions are used exclusively for local processing. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
