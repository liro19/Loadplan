FAQ

Is there a limit to how many aircraft I can track? ↓

No. LoadPlan allows you to manage an unlimited fleet of aircraft with individual maintenance logs and weight configurations.

Can I share my load plans? ↓

Yes. All load plans can be exported as professional PDFs and shared via the native iOS Share Sheet or saved directly to the Files app.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

At LoadPlan, we believe your flight data should belong to you. Our app is built on a "local-first" philosophy. This means your weight and balance calculations, maintenance logs, and aircraft profiles are stored directly on your device using SwiftData.

We do not use any external servers to store or process your sensitive operational data. Because your data stays on your Mac, iPhone, or iPad, we cannot see, access, or share it with any third parties.

By using LoadPlan, you agree that data management and backup (via iCloud or local device backups) are handled through your own Apple account. We do not track your location, your flight paths, or your specific weight data.

Your safety is your responsibility. While LoadPlan provides high-precision calculations, always verify final load plans against your Pilot's Operating Handbook (POH).
